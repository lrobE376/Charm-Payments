PRICING QUESTIONS

Common Questions About Our Rates and Fees

Interchange-plus (IC+) passes the card network's base cost directly to you plus a small fixed markup. You see exactly what Visa or Mastercard charges and exactly what Charm Payments charges — the most transparent pricing model in the industry.

No monthly fees on Starter or Growth. No setup fees on any plan. Your complete Schedule of Fees is provided before you sign anything. The only costs are per-transaction rates and standard card network fees.

Most standard merchant accounts are approved within 24–48 business hours. High-risk accounts may take 3–5 business days. Once approved we configure your gateway and you can start processing the same day.

Card transactions typically settle to your bank within 1–2 business days. ACH transactions follow Nacha settlement timelines, generally 1–3 business days. Settlement timing is outlined in your Merchant Agreement.

No long-term contracts on Starter or Growth — both are month-to-month. Enterprise accounts may include negotiated term agreements clearly disclosed before signing.

A chargeback is a dispute initiated by a cardholder with their bank. A chargeback fee applies as disclosed in your fee schedule and the disputed amount is temporarily held. Our team notifies you immediately and assists you in submitting evidence to dispute it. Outcomes are determined by the card network.
